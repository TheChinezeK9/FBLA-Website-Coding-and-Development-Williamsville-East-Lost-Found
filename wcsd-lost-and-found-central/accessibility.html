Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making Williamsville East High School Lost & Found usable and accessible for all students, families, and staff.

1. Accessibility Commitment
We strive to provide clear navigation, readable text, and accessible interactions throughout the site for keyboard and assistive technology users.

2. Inclusive Design Practices
We design and test core flows to support responsive layouts, visible controls, and predictable behavior across common desktop and mobile environments.

3. Browser and Device Support
Accessibility can vary by browser, extension, or assistive tool. If you encounter an issue, we will review and improve it as quickly as possible.

4. Feedback and Assistance
If you need help using this website or want to report an accessibility barrier, please contact the Williamsville East team so we can provide support and make corrections.
